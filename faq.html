Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Movara Shopping?

Movara Shopping is an online shopping platform operated by Movara Industries, offering groceries, spices and other daily essentials.


2. Do you deliver all over India?

Delivery availability depends on the service area. We aim to expand our delivery network over time.


3. Which payment methods are accepted?

We may support UPI, Debit Cards, Credit Cards, Net Banking and Cash on Delivery where available.


4. How can I track my order?

After your order is shipped, you will receive tracking details through email or SMS, if available.


5. Can I cancel my order?

Yes, orders can usually be cancelled before shipment. Once shipped, cancellation may not be possible.


6. What if I receive a damaged or wrong product?

Please contact our customer support with photos of the product. We will review your request according to our Return & Refund Policy.
