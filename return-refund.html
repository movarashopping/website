Return & Refund Policy

1. Return Eligibility

Products may be eligible for return if they are received in a damaged, defective or incorrect condition.


2. Return Request

Return requests should be submitted within 7 days of receiving the order along with photos or videos of the product.


3. Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened or used food products.
  • Products damaged due to misuse.
  • Items marked as non-returnable.

4. Refund Process

After the returned product is inspected and approved, the refund will be processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.


5. Replacement

If the product is damaged, defective or incorrect, we may provide a replacement instead of a refund where applicable.


6. Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped. Once shipped, cancellation may not be possible.


7. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are generally non-refundable unless the return is due to our mistake or a damaged/incorrect product.
